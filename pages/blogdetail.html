Acne Treatment Guide To Have Better Skin

Core Skincare Routine (Daily) To Reduced Your Acnes

LAXIA Admin • April 13, 2026 • 7 min read

Korean skincare focuses on hydration, prevention, and gentle care rather than harsh treatments.

What is Korean Skincare?

It is a routine based on layering lightweight products to improve skin health over time.

Healthy skin is built, not covered.

Know Your Skin Type

  • • Oily skin – shiny and acne-prone
  • • Dry skin – tight and flaky
  • • Combination – mixed zones
  • • Sensitive – easily irritated

Core Routine

Cleanser

Use a gentle cleanser that does not strip your skin.

Toner

Hydrates and prepares your skin.

Serum

Treats specific skin concerns.

Moisturizer + SPF

Locks hydration and protects your skin.

Final Thoughts

Start simple and build consistency. That is the real secret of skincare.
